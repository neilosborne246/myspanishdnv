For faster answers

What to include in your enquiry

These four details let us give you a useful answer straight away — without having to chase you for more information.

1

Your nationality

Non-EU/EEA nationals apply through the consulate route. EU citizens living in Spain apply through UGE directly. Knowing your nationality lets us give you the right guidance.

2

Current country of residence

If you're applying from abroad, you'll go through a Spanish consulate. If you're already in Spain, you apply via UGE. This changes the timeline and documentation required.

3

Employed or self-employed?

The DNV is open to both remote employees and registered freelancers (autónomos). The required documents differ depending on your employment type, so this helps us tailor our advice.

4

Approximate monthly income

The 2026 income requirement is €2,849 per month (200% of Spain's SMI). Letting us know your earnings helps us confirm eligibility and flag anything worth discussing.

How it works

From first click to visa approved

A clear, guided process — every step handled through our platform, with your case manager available throughout.

01

Eligibility quiz

Register and take our free eligibility quiz. Find out in minutes whether you qualify for the DNV — no commitment required.

02

Free call with a specialist

Book a free call with one of our immigration specialists. We'll confirm your route, answer your questions, and make sure you're ready to proceed.

03

Open your case

Start on the platform. Pay Stage 1 (€500) and your secure dashboard opens immediately — your dedicated case manager is assigned straight away.

04

Upload your documents

We send you a personalised document checklist. Upload each item directly in the platform, tick them off as you go, and track progress in real time.

05

We translate & submit

We handle all certified translations and prepare your full application dossier. Once ready, we submit on your behalf — you pay Stage 2 at this point.

06

Visa approved

We receive your visa decision and notify you immediately. Final payment (Stage 3, €899) is only due on approval. Your case manager is available throughout — on the platform or via video call.
